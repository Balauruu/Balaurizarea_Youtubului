## Voice & Style Rules

1. **No clickbait inflation** — Titles must be accurate. If something is disturbing, it should be disturbing on its own merits.
2. **Script is narration only** — No host commentary, no "smash that like button," no fourth-wall breaks.
3. **Chapters structure the story** — Every video has numbered acts. The audience always knows where they are.
4. **Sources are sacred** — Every claim must be sourced. Speculation is labeled as such.
5. **Pacing through silence** — Let heavy information land. Don't rush past the horror.
6. **Open endings are valid** — If a mystery is unsolved, we present the evidence and leave the audience with the weight of not knowing.
