## Topic Brief

## 1. The Duplessis Orphans: Quebec's Stolen Children

**Pillar:** Institutional Corruption & Cover-ups

**Score:** O:4 C:5 S:5 V:4 = 18/20  |  ~40min

**Hook:** For decades, the Catholic Church and Quebec government conspired to falsely certify thousands of healthy orphans as mentally ill — locking them in psychiatric institutions for higher federal subsidies, where they were subjected to abuse, forced labor, and medical experimentation.

**Timeline:**
- 1940s: Quebec Premier Maurice Duplessis strikes deal with Catholic Church to reclassify orphans as psychiatric patients
- 1940s-1960s: Over 20,000 orphans falsely certified as mentally deficient and transferred to psychiatric institutions
- 1950s: Reports of systematic physical abuse, sexual abuse, and forced labor emerge but are suppressed
- 1960s: Quiet Revolution ends Church control of Quebec social services; institutions close
- 1990s: Survivors organize as the Duplessis Orphans Committee and begin public campaign for justice
- 1999: Quebec government offers limited compensation but refuses full inquiry
- 2004: Survivors petition to exhume suspected mass graves at former institutions; request denied
- 2021: Renewed calls for investigation after discovery of unmarked graves at Canadian residential schools
