Old Cartoon Sources (Specific)

  • archive.org/details/19201928publicdomainanimation — 1920-1928 silent cartoons collection
  • publicdomainreview.org/collections/all/genre/animation/ — Curated PD animations (Fantasmagorie 1908, Gertie 1914, etc.)
  • loc.gov National Film Registry PD list — Includes early Popeye, Fleischer-era cartoons
  • Wikimedia Commons animated shorts category — Mutt and Jeff, Felix the Cat, others
  • Caveat: Always verify soundtracks are also PD — video may be PD but added music may not be

YouTube (via yt-dlp)

For downloading Creative Commons licensed footage and old cartoons uploaded to YouTube. The pipeline already has yt-dlp as a skill. Use for:

  • Public domain cartoons re-uploaded to YouTube
  • CC-licensed b-roll channels
  • Historical footage compilations (verify PD status)
