## Competitor Analysis

### Fredrik Knudsen — @FredrikKnudsen
- **1.26M subscribers | 39 videos | 111.4M total views**
- **Style:** Clinically neutral, deadpan, near-zero editorializing. Reads source text verbatim. Newsreader composure regardless of content.
- **Format:** Deep dives into weird internet history and obscure real-world events. 20–60 min.
- **What works:** The neutrality creates unease. The depth creates trust. Audience feels like they're discovering something hidden.
- **Top topics:** Mouse Utopia Experiments, Austrian Wine Poisoning, Collyer Brothers — niche, esoteric, historically grounded.
- **Lesson:** Objectivity is a stylistic weapon. Don't editorialize — let the facts horrify the audience themselves.

### Unnamed TV — @unnamedTV

- **Lesson:** Quality over quantity. A single well-crafted story beats a flood of mediocre content. Cults and historical crime convert well.
